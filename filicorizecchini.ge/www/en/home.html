About Filicori Zecchini

For over a century, Filicori Zecchini has been crafting exceptional coffee experiences in the heart of Italy. Our journey began in Bologna in 1919, where passion for quality and tradition created a legacy that continues to inspire coffee lovers worldwide.

Today, we bring the authentic taste of Italian coffee culture to Georgia, offering premium coffee beans, capsules, and professional coffee machines that transform every cup into a moment of pure indulgence.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond our products – we provide comprehensive support, training, and maintenance services to ensure your coffee journey is nothing short of extraordinary.
