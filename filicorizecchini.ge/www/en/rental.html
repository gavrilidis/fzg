How Our Rental Process Works

  1. Initial Consultation: Discuss your needs, timeline, and budget with our rental specialists
  2. Equipment Selection: Choose the perfect equipment combination for your requirements
  3. Quote & Agreement: Receive a detailed quote and sign the rental agreement
  4. Delivery & Setup: Professional delivery and installation at your location
  5. Training & Support: Staff training and ongoing technical support throughout the rental
  6. Pickup or Purchase: Equipment pickup at the end of rental or option to purchase

Rental Requirements & Information

What We Need From You

  • Business Information: Company details, contact information, and business license
  • Location Details: Address, access information, and power/water specifications
  • Timeline: Start date, duration, and any specific scheduling requirements
  • Staff Information: Number of people who will operate the equipment
  • Insurance: Proof of business liability insurance (we can provide options)

What We Provide

  • Equipment: Professionally maintained, fully tested coffee machines and accessories
  • Installation: Complete setup and configuration at your location
  • Training: Comprehensive staff training on equipment operation and maintenance
  • Support: 24/7 technical support and emergency service
  • Maintenance: Regular cleaning and maintenance visits
  • Supplies: Initial supply of coffee, filters, and cleaning materials
