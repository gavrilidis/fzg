About Filicori Zecchini

A century of Italian coffee excellence, now bringing the authentic taste of premium coffee to Georgia

Our Story

Founded in 1919 in Bologna, Italy, Filicori Zecchini has been at the forefront of coffee innovation for over a century. What began as a small family roastery has grown into one of Italy's most respected coffee brands, known for exceptional quality and unwavering commitment to tradition.

Our journey to Georgia represents a natural expansion of our mission to share the authentic Italian coffee experience with coffee lovers around the world. We bring not just coffee, but a piece of Italian culture and heritage to every cup.

Our Mission & Values

Uncompromising Quality

We source only the finest Arabica beans from sustainable farms and roast them to perfection using traditional Italian methods passed down through generations.

Heritage & Tradition

Our century-old roasting techniques and family recipes ensure that every cup carries the authentic taste of Italian coffee culture.

Innovation & Excellence

While honoring tradition, we continuously innovate in coffee technology and service delivery to meet modern business needs.

Sustainability & Ethics

We are committed to ethical sourcing, environmental responsibility, and supporting coffee-growing communities worldwide.

Global Presence

Filicori Zecchini serves coffee lovers in over 30 countries worldwide. Our expansion to Georgia represents our commitment to bringing premium Italian coffee to emerging markets.

From our headquarters in Bologna to our new operations center in Tbilisi, we maintain the same high standards and authentic Italian coffee experience that has made us a global leader in the industry.
